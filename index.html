We Support people

Lyndale is a charity providing support to people with cancer and their family in a warm and friendly environment

Lyndale is based in Huyton, Knowsley however support is available to people from all over Merseyside and the Wirral. No referral is necessary you can just drop-in, give us a call or email us

Lyndale was founded in 1983 by a local resident and has built strong links with all the local hospitals and hospices, GP's and other health professionals.

WHAT WE PROVIDE

We provide one to one support, group support, free complementary therapies, yoga and relaxation sessions. Our volunteers organise days out, fundraising activities, theatre trips and more. We apply for grants to enable us to provide therapies, craft and art classes which renews people's confidence and wellbeing. People make friends as they share their journey with cancer and the treatments while also supporting each other.
